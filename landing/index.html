FAQ
Common questions
Is my Aadhaar and PAN data safe? ▼
Yes. All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM directly in your browser before being stored. Your encryption key is derived from your master password using PBKDF2 with 300,000 iterations. We never see your data — not even your password.
Does the autofill work on all government sites? ▼
The extension works on 50+ major portals including SSC, UPSC, Railway RRB, NTA, IBPS, SBI, and most state PSC portals. If a specific portal doesn't work, contact us and we'll add support within 48 hours.
What if I forget my master password? ▼
Because of zero-knowledge encryption, we cannot recover your password. However, you can reset the vault and re-enter your data. We recommend noting your master password safely offline.
